She is a Research Assistant for the Opportunity & Inclusive Growth Institute at the Federal Reserve Bank of Minneapolis. Her teaching fields are: Microeconomics (JEL D), Econometrics (JEL C), and Labor Economics (JEL J). Her areas of interest are: Economics of Minorities/Gender (JEL J15/J16), Equity, Justice, and Inequality (JEL D63), Education and Inequality (JEL I24), and Stratification Economics (JEL Z13).
